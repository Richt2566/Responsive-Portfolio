About Me

I am an aspiring artist, photographer, designer and web developer. I have studied English at the college of Lake County and received an Associate of Arts degree. I am currently studying full stack web development at Northwestern Coding Bootcamp.

I am passionate about music, film, art, literature; anything that incorporates creativity. I hope to create beautiful websites that feature some of the subjects above. I hope to create applications that encourage open-minded learning and experimentation.
